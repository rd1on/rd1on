    Narrow AI

    • Designed and trained to perform a specific task.
    • Operates under a limited pre-defined set of conditions.

    Artificial General Intelligence (AGI)

    • Have the ability to understand, learn and apply knowledge.
    • Capable of self-awareness, reasoning, and emotional understanding.

    Artificial Super Intelligence

    • Surpass human intelligence in nearly all aspects.
    • Exists more in the realm of science fiction and future speculation than in current reality.
